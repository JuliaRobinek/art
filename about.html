about

Painter. Meditation teacher. Web designer. Value sensitive. Once an economist. You get the idea. Life is an unfolding story.

As a painter, she captures life's vibrant variations, portraying the world as a shifting dreamscape where reality flows like water. Her art explores the transient nature of existence, reflecting our ever-changing consciousness, where memories and dreams blur together.

Nature, especially water, is central to her work, symbolizing life's fluidity and the interconnectedness of all things. She sees the external landscape as a mirror of the inner one, where emotions and thoughts subtly shape every scene.

At the core of her art is the journey of the self—a timeless exploration of the inner voice that propels us forward or pulls us into the shadows of the past. Her paintings act as portals to these inner worlds, blending the familiar with the otherworldly.

For her, life is an unfolding story of beauty, mystery, and transformation. Through her art, she invites us into a space where reality and imagination merge, making the journey as meaningful as the destination.
