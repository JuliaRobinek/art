about

Painter. Meditation teacher. Web designer. Value sensitive. Once an economist. You get the idea.

As a painter she captures life in all its vibrant variations, seeing the world as a vast, colorful dreamscape where reality constantly shifts and flows like water. She explores the transilient nature of existence—where one moment dissolves into the next, and nothing remains fixed. Her art reflects the ever-changing reality of our consciousness, the way we drift between memories and dreams, often unsure of where one begins and the other ends.

Nature, especially water, is a recurring motif in her work—a reminder of the fluidity of life, of how everything is interconnected, always in motion. She views the world as a place where the external landscape mirrors the inner one, where every scene is infused with the thoughts and emotions that flow beneath the surface.

At the heart of her work is the journey of the self—the timeless hero's journey. Through her art, she delves into the voice in the head, that inner dialogue we all carry, sometimes leading us forward, other times pulling us back into the shadows of our past. Her paintings become portals to these inner worlds, where memories and dreams intermingle, where every figure and landscape is both familiar and otherworldly.

In her eyes, life is not a static picture but an unfolding story—one filled with beauty, mystery, and endless transformation. Through her art, she invites to a place where reality and imagination blur, where the journey is as important as the destination, and where every moment is a fleeting, vivid dream waiting to be painted.
